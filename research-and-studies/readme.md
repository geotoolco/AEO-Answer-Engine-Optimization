# Research & Studies

The most cited data on AEO, AI citation patterns, and the impact of AI search on organic traffic.

---

## Foundational Research

**[GEO: Generative Engine Optimization — Princeton/Georgia Tech/Allen Institute](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09735)**
The paper most AEO strategies trace back to. Shows 40% visibility improvements from specific tactics: adding statistics, citing sources, and including expert quotations. The most rigorous academic evidence base available.

**[Conductor AEO/GEO Benchmarks Report](https://www.conductor.com/academy/aeo-geo-benchmarks-report/)**
First large-scale industry analysis: 13,770 domains, 21.9M searches, 17M AI responses. Key finding: ChatGPT drives 87.4% of AI referral traffic. AI referral conversions run 2× higher than traditional organic.

**[Profound AI Platform Citation Patterns](https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/ai-platform-citation-patterns)**
Analysis of 30M citations: Wikipedia accounts for 47.9% of ChatGPT's top citations; Reddit leads Perplexity and Google AI Overviews. Platform-specific data — useful for prioritizing where to build authority.

---

## Key Stats

**[Ahrefs AI Overviews CTR Study](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-overviews-reduce-clicks/)**
300,000 keyword analysis: position 1 CTR drops 58% when an AI Overview is present. The clearest data on why AEO is no longer optional for brands that depend on organic search.

**[AI Traffic Surges 527% in 2025](https://www.superprompt.com/blog/ai-traffic-up-527-percent-how-to-get-cited-by-chatgpt-claude-perplexity-2025)**
Analysis from 400+ sites. AI-referred sessions grew 527% year-over-year in the first half of 2025.

**[State of AI Search Optimization 2026 — Kevin Indig](https://www.growth-memo.com/p/state-of-ai-search-optimization-2026)**
Content under 3 months old is 3× more likely to be cited. 24% of ChatGPT responses generated without fetching any online content — the knowledge cutoff gap brands can't optimize past.
