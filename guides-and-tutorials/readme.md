## What actually works

The Princeton/IIT Delhi [GEO research paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09735) is the most cited evidence base — 40% visibility improvements from specific tactics: adding statistics, citing sources, including direct expert quotes. Most guides trace back to these findings.

The practical priorities: answer-first content structure, named authorship, third-party mentions on high-authority domains (Reddit, G2, Wikipedia), and consistent entity data across the web.
